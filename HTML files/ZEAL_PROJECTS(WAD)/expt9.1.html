PETROL ENGINE vs DIESEL ENGINE
Petrol Engine Diesel Engine Definition
1) Uses petrol as fuel. 1) Uses diesel as fuel. Internal Combustion Engine:

An engine in which the combustion of fuel occurs inside the engine cylinder, producing power by converting chemical energy into mechanical energy. Petrol and diesel engines are the most common types of ICEs used in vehicles.
2) Uses spark plug for ignition. 2) Uses self-ignition due to high compression.
3) Operates at higher RPM. 3) Produces high torque at lower RPM.
