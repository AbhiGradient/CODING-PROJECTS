Modern India

Colonial rule and the struggle for independence.

British Rule

British colonial rule (1757–1947) brought railways, legal systems, and administrative changes, but also economic hardship and social upheaval. Education and trade were influenced by European ideas.

Independence Movement

Leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Jawaharlal Nehru, and Subhas Chandra Bose inspired millions in the fight for freedom. Non-violent protests, civil disobedience, and mass movements led to India gaining independence in 1947.
