Medieval India

Era of kingdoms, empires, and cultural fusion.

Delhi Sultanate

The Delhi Sultanate (1206–1526 CE) established Islamic rule in northern India, introducing new architecture, culture, and administrative systems. Trade and urban centers expanded, influencing art and society.

Mughal Empire

The Mughal Empire (1526–1857 CE) brought cultural brilliance, monumental architecture, and centralized governance. Emperors like Akbar and Shah Jahan patronized art, literature, and religion, shaping the rich cultural heritage of India.
